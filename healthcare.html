Medical Imaging

This is the technique of scanning organs and/ or bones in the body to generate images for medical use, it can be done by:

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Ultrasound
  • Radiography
  • Magnetic Particle Imaging
  • Elestography
